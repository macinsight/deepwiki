DEEPNET is a browser-based hacking simulation. No download — you open <https://deepnet.us/> and you're at a terminal. Everything happens through text commands.

The Core Loop
Scan → Hack → Breach → Exfil → Sell.

Scan a subnet, find targets with different services and security levels. Pick an exploit that matches — SSH, FTP, HTTP, MySQL, and 14 more service types. Good match = low risk. Bad match = you get caught.

Once inside, you enter the Breach Engine. Navigate a generated filesystem, read files, decide what to steal. Every action generates noise. At 100% trace — force-disconnect, everything lost. Exfil data, backdoors, credentials — gone.

Get out clean, sell your data, upgrade your rig, craft better tools, go again.

The World Reacts
This isn't static. Your actions have consequences across interconnected systems:

Heat — Hack too much and the network notices. Targets get harder, payouts shrink, NPCs post about you. Back off and it decays.
Target Lifecycle — Targets get stressed, go offline, sometimes die permanently. New ones replace them.
NPC Trust — 21 AI-powered NPCs with their own interests. They remember you.
Factions — COVENANT vs. MERIDIAN. Permanent choice. Changes difficulty, trust, and heat.
Geopolitics — World cycles through states (STABLE → CRISIS → DETENTE). Players can push it into crisis by hacking government targets.
Shadow Profile — The network builds a dossier on you. Eventually you get a codename. Permanent.
Tools & Crafting
18-service matching matrix. Tools have Power, Noise, Stability, Opsec stats. Craft them via DeepComp (compiler) and DeepAI (AI forge). Tools wear out.

Multiplayer
PvP hacking (steal RCH from other players), crews, player-to-player contracts, timed bounties. New players protected by Newblood Shield.

How It Compares
vs. Hacknet: Hacknet is story-driven, play once. DEEPNET is systems-driven, ongoing, living world.
vs. Uplink: Closest relative. DEEPNET adds multiplayer, factions, geopolitics, AI NPCs, post-exploitation breach engine.

Current State
Solo project. Playable, deep, rough around the edges. Free. In active development.
